Digital Oxford Township (University of Iowa Dept of History)

Research project from Digital History Workshop class at the University of Iowa.

Utilizes public history archives and digital resources to detail the development of Oxford, IA.

Compares and contrasts Oxford's social, cultural, and economic themes to the regional and global themes of the 19th and 20th centuries.
