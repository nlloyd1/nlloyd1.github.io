About Me

I am an undergraduate computer science major from Johnston, Iowa. Before transferring to Iowa State University, I completed the first three semesters of my Bachelor's degree at the University of Iowa, where I held a second major in history.

My primary interests in the computer science field are cybersecurity and cryptography. I plan to continue studying the history and modern day applications of these topics through coursework, research, and independent projects. After completing my Bachelor's degree, I hope to attend graduate school to widen my research experience in this field.
